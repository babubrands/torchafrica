Who We Are

Advocacy with a justice-first lens.

Torch Africa is a civil justice platform focused on human rights, constitutional accountability, and public-interest advocacy. The organization brings together legal insight, civic education, documentation, and public engagement to support communities and institutions working toward a fairer society.
